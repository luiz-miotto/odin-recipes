Boiled Eggs

Description

Boiled eggs are...just that. They're eggs that are boiled. You can throw some salt on them and go to town!

Ingredients

  • eggs

Steps

  1. Filll a pot with faucet water
  2. Boil water on stove using a medium-high heat
  3. Once water is boiling, carefully place eggs in water
  4. Boil for 8-10 minutes
  5. Pull eggs from water
  6. Remove the egg shells from the eggs
  7. Rinse egg to remove sheel debris
  8. Enjoy with light salt!
